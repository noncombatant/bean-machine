Searching

Bean Machine’s search feature is fairly simple.

  • james brown will match all items that contain both “james” and “brown”, anywhere in their metadata. This includes substrings, so a hypothetical artist named Jameson Brownington will also match. This also means that a hypothetical record named Brown by the band James would match.
  • "james brown" will match all items that contain the phrase anywhere in their metadata. Jameson Brownington and Brown by James no longer match, although James Brownington still would.
  • You can also prefix terms with - to negate them: "james brown" -popcorn matches all items that contain the phrase anywhere in their metadata, but will exclude items that match “popcorn”.
